Dachs Ice – Website Planning Document

1. Site DachsIce Sorveteria

Sorvetes – Milkshake – Açaí – Bubble Waffle

I chose this name because it represents the identity of the ice cream kiosk and is easy for customers to recognize. It allows for fun, colorful branding that matches the product theme.

Optional domain: dachsice.com

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of the Dachs Ice website is to present the ice cream kiosk, showcase its products, list prices, and allow users to place online orders. The site will also provide customer registration, functional navigation, and full contact information including address and business hours.

3. Scenarios

  • What products does Dachs Ice offer, and how much does each item cost?
  • How can I create and submit an online order for ice cream, milkshakes, or açaí?

4. Color Scheme

Selected colors:

  • Light Blue (#6ECFF6) – used for accents, header highlight areas, buttons, and section dividers.
  • Pink (#FF6CA8) – used in headings, highlights, and call-to-action elements.
  • White (#FFFFFF) – used as the main page background to maintain clarity and contrast.

These colors match the fun and refreshing visual identity of an ice cream brand.

5. Typography

  • Montserrat – used for titles and headings.
  • Poppins – used for paragraphs and general content.

This combination provides readability while keeping a modern, friendly look appropriate for a dessert-themed brand.
